#+title: Scheme: unload-feature
#+filetags: :scheme:api:modules:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(unload-feature NAME)
#+end_src

Mark a feature as unloaded in the package system.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(unload-feature "my-package")
#+end_src
