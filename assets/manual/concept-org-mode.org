#+title: Org-mode
#+filetags: :org:editing:

**Org-mode** in MAE provides structural editing and task management capabilities for `.org` files, inspired by Doom Emacs evil-org.

** Core Features

*** 1. Three-State Heading Cycle (TAB)
Press `TAB` on a heading to cycle its visibility through three states:
**Subtree** (everything visible) -> **Folded** (heading only) -> **Children** (body + child headings visible, child bodies folded) -> **Subtree**.
Leaf headings (no children) toggle between **Subtree** and **Folded**.

*** 2. Fold All / Unfold All
- `zM` (`close-all-folds`): Fold all headings in the buffer.
- `zR` (`open-all-folds`): Unfold all headings.
- `za`: Toggle fold at cursor (tree-sitter or heading).

*** 3. Structural Editing
- `M-h` / `M-Left`: **Promote** heading (remove one `*` prefix).
- `M-l` / `M-Right`: **Demote** heading (add one `*` prefix).
- `M-k` / `M-Up`: **Move subtree up** past previous sibling.
- `M-j` / `M-Down`: **Move subtree down** past next sibling.
Moving a subtree automatically clears any folds in the affected range.

*** 4. Narrow / Widen
- `SPC m s n` (`org-narrow-subtree`): Narrow buffer to current heading's subtree. Only lines in that subtree are visible; cursor is clamped to the range. Status bar shows `[Narrowed]`.
- `SPC m s w` (`org-widen`): Restore full buffer visibility.

*** 5. Heading Font Scaling
Org headings render at scaled font sizes for visual hierarchy:
`*` = 1.5x, `**` = 1.3x, `***` = 1.15x.
Disable with `:set heading_scale false`.

*** 6. Insert Heading (M-Enter)
- On a heading line: Insert a new heading at the **same level** after the current subtree.
- Not on a heading: Insert a level-1 heading below the current line.
- Automatically enters insert mode with cursor after the heading prefix.

*** 7. Task Management
- `S-Left` / `S-Right`: Cycle TODO states (`TODO` -> `DONE` -> `None`).
- `S-Up` / `S-Down`: Cycle priorities (`[#A]` -> `[#B]` -> `[#C]`).

*** 8. Links
Press `Enter` on a link to follow it. Internal links jump to headings; external links open in your browser.

*** 9. Rich Rendering
- `*bold*` text is rendered in bold.
- `/italic/` text is rendered in italics.
- **Emphasis Markers**: Use `:set org_hide_emphasis_markers true` to hide the surrounding `*` and `/` characters.
