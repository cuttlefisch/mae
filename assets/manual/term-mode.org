#+title: Mode
#+filetags: :terminology:

Current editing state: Normal, Insert, Visual (char/line/block), Command, Search, ShellInsert, FilePicker, FileBrowser, CommandPalette, ConversationInput. Not to be confused with Emacs major-modes -- MAE modes are vi-style modal states.
