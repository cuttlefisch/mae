#+title: Scheme: string-trim
#+filetags: :scheme:api:string-utils:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(string-trim STR)
#+end_src

Remove leading and trailing whitespace from STR.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(string-trim "  hello  ") ; => "hello"
#+end_src
