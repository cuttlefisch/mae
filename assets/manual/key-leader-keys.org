** SPC Leader Bindings (Doom Emacs style)

MAE uses ~SPC~ as leader in normal mode, organized into 14 groups.
Press ~SPC~ to see the which-key popup showing available sub-keys.

*** SPC SPC — Command Palette
Fuzzy-search all commands (like Doom's ~M-x~ or VSCode's ~Ctrl-Shift-P~).

*** SPC / — Project Search
Quick shortcut for ~project-search~ (ripgrep in project root).
