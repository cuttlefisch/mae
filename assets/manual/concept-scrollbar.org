#+title: Scrollbar
#+filetags: :gui:rendering:

**Vertical scrollbar** for the GUI rendering backend.

** Configuration
- `:set scrollbar true` (default: enabled)
- `:set scrollbar false` to disable

** Layout
The scrollbar occupies 1 column at the right edge of the text area. Space is allocated in `FrameLayout::compute_layout()` *before* wrap/layout computation, so text wrapping respects the reduced width.

** Rendering
- **Track**: full content-area height, theme color `ui.scrollbar.track`
- **Thumb**: proportional to viewport/total ratio, theme color `ui.scrollbar.thumb`
- Minimum thumb height: 1 cell

** Mouse Interaction
Click in the scrollbar column to jump to that scroll position.

** Nyan Mode
`:set nyan_mode true` enables a rainbow progress bar in the status line, showing scroll position as a filled bar with a cat marker.
