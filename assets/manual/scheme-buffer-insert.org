#+title: Scheme: buffer-insert
#+filetags: :scheme:api:buffer-editing:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(buffer-insert TEXT)
#+end_src

Insert TEXT at the current cursor position.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(buffer-insert "hello world")
#+end_src
