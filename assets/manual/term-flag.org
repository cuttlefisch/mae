#+title: Flag
#+filetags: :terminology:

An optional sub-feature of a module, enabled with =+name= syntax in the =mae!= block. Flags are declared in =module.toml= and checked in Scheme with =(when-flag "+name" thunk)=. Example: =(org +agenda +babel)=.
