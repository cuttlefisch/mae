#+title: Scheme: should-not
#+filetags: :scheme:api:testing:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(should-not VAL)
#+end_src

Assert VAL is falsy. Signals an error on failure.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(should-not (= 1 2))
#+end_src
