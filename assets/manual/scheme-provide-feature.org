#+title: Scheme: provide-feature
#+filetags: :scheme:api:packages:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(provide-feature FEATURE)
#+end_src

Mark FEATURE as loaded in the package system.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(provide-feature "my-package")
#+end_src
