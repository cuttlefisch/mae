#+title: Scheme: kill-buffer-by-name

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(kill-buffer-by-name NAME)
#+end_src

Close the buffer matching NAME.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(kill-buffer-by-name "*scratch*")
#+end_src
