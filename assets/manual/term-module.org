#+title: Module
#+filetags: :terminology:

A self-contained package of commands, keybindings, hooks, and options. Modules have a =module.toml= manifest, =autoloads.scm= (eager registration), and =init.scm= (lazy initialization). Modules are Scheme-only packages; Rust-level changes go through the kernel.
