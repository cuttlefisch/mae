#+title: Scheme: undefine-key!
#+filetags: :scheme:api:keymaps:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(undefine-key! MAP KEY)
#+end_src

Remove a key binding from a keymap.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(undefine-key! "normal" "q")
#+end_src
