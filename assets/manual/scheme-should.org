#+title: Scheme: should
#+filetags: :scheme:api:testing:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(should VAL)
#+end_src

Assert VAL is truthy. Signals an error on failure.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(should (> 3 2))
#+end_src
