#+title: Scheme: require-feature
#+filetags: :scheme:api:packages:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(require-feature FEATURE)
#+end_src

Request loading of FEATURE from the load-path.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(require-feature "my-package")
#+end_src
