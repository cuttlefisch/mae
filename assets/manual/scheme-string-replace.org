#+title: Scheme: string-replace
#+filetags: :scheme:api:string-utils:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(string-replace STR OLD NEW)
#+end_src

Replace all occurrences of OLD with NEW in STR.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(string-replace "foo bar foo" "foo" "baz") ; => "baz bar baz"
#+end_src
