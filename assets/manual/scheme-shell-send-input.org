#+title: Scheme: shell-send-input
#+filetags: :scheme:api:shell:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(shell-send-input BUF-IDX TEXT)
#+end_src

Send TEXT to the PTY of a terminal buffer.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(shell-send-input 1 "ls\n")
#+end_src
