** Lesson 3: Editing

Prerequisites: [[lesson:modes?rel=requires][Lesson 2: Modes]]

This lesson [[concept:command?rel=teaches][teaches editing commands]].

*** Inserting text
  ~i~ — insert before cursor    ~a~ — insert after cursor
  ~o~ — open line below         ~O~ — open line above

*** Deleting text
  ~x~ — delete character         [[cmd:delete-line][dd]] — delete line
  ~dw~ — delete word             ~d$~ — delete to end of line

*** Undo / Redo
  [[cmd:undo][u]] — undo          ~Ctrl-r~ — redo

*** Clipboard
  ~yy~ — yank (copy) line       ~p~ — paste after
  ~P~ — paste before

*** Repeat
  ~.~ — repeat last edit
