#+title: Scheme: remove-hook!
#+filetags: :scheme:api:hooks:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(remove-hook! HOOK-NAME FN-NAME)
#+end_src

Remove a function from a hook.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(remove-hook! "buffer-open" "my-on-open")
#+end_src
