#+title: Scheme: advice-add!
#+filetags: :scheme:api:advice:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(advice-add! COMMAND WHERE FN-NAME)
#+end_src

Add advice to a command. WHERE is :before or :after.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(advice-add! "save" :before "my-before-save")
#+end_src
