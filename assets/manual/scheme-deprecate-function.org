#+title: Scheme: deprecate-function!
#+filetags: :scheme:api:modules:

** Signature
#+begin_src scheme
(deprecate-function! OLD-NAME NEW-NAME)
#+end_src

Mark OLD-NAME as deprecated in favor of NEW-NAME. Emits a warning on use.

** Example
#+begin_src scheme
(deprecate-function! "old-fn" "new-fn")
#+end_src
